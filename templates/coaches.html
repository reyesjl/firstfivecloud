Certifications

World Rugby Level 1 Coach (In-Person)

Access Course

Designed for individuals coaching rugby involving tackling, World Rugby Level 1 Coach is suitable for those aged 17 and above. This 8-hour in-person course focuses on player welfare, technical skills, and effective coaching techniques. It also includes online courses on World Rugby's Passport website to complete before certification.

World Rugby Level 2 Coach (In-Person)

Access Course

Intended for Level 1 Coaches with a year of experience, World Rugby Level 2 Coach is ideal for coaching adult players aged 18 or older. This 2-day, 16-hour in-person course explores advanced coaching skills, including athlete development and program building. It involves pre-coursework, an in-person clinic, and post-coursework, often taking a year or more to complete.

World Rugby Level 3 Coach (In-Person)

Access Course

Designed for aspiring or professional rugby coaches, World Rugby Level 3 Coach is a comprehensive year-long program. It includes multiple online sessions, a 3-day in-person camp, and an in-person assessment. This course is by application, with applications opening in the summer and an annual intake beginning in August.
