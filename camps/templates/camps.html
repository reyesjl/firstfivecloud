Overview. Rugby Skills Camps

Join our Rugby Skills Camps held throughout the year. Each camp is designed to provide individual coaching on core skills, with hundreds of reps and skill-building progression focusing on field vision, spacing, and timing. All participants will receive a CCC tee and be entered to win CCC prizes daily.

Camp Highlights. What to expect.

  • Individual coaching on core skills
  • Skill-building progression focusing on field vision, spacing, and timing
  • Feedback and evaluation shared with your team coach
  • Prizes and raffles daily

Our Esteemed Staff. Meet our team.

Our camps are run by a team of experienced and dedicated rugby professionals, including Directors of Rugby, Community Rugby experts, operations managers, and MLR players. Our staff have experience at all levels of the game and are passionate about helping players improve.

Important Information. Need-to-knows.

  • Camps are open to all coaches and boys aged 14-18 (specific camps for women's rugby coming soon)
  • Proof of current insurance is required
  • For any questions, please email rugbyvarsity@gmail.com

Location and Schedule. When and where.

Our camps are held at different times throughout the year at various locations. Each camp typically runs for several days, with intensive training sessions held each day.

Registration. Reserve your spot.

  • Registration is typically limited, so be sure to secure your spot early!
  • Upon registration, you will be sent an email with medical forms and payment details.
  • Please note that all releases must be filled out and payment made in full to be registered.

Cost. Invest in your skills.

The cost for players and coaches varies per camp. Please refer to specific camp details for pricing.
